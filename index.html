About I&I Sound

We build high-fidelity, sculptural sound systems that make audio visible and celebrated. Our mission: to honor dub culture while redefining live audio as both performance and art.

Technical Innovation

Speaker Construction

Birch plywood cabinets braced for resonance-free clarity. Drivers optimized per frequency band. Active DSP and Class D amplification deliver peak performance.

Acoustic Design

Modular sub/mid/high enclosures tuned for perfect balance. Crossover, phase alignment, and EQ precision create immersive live experiences.

Finishing & Branding

Art Deco-inspired lines, gold accents, CNC-cut logos, and matte finishes integrate visual identity without compromising acoustics.

Design Philosophy

Each system is a sculptural object. Bold geometry, Art Deco influence, and Joel Siegel-inspired minimalism make every speaker a visual centerpiece. Portability and modularity ensure stage-ready deployment.

Events & Community
